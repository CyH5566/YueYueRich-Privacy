# Privacy Policy for YueYueRich (月月富)

Last updated: February 24, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to YueYueRich. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

## 2. Third-Party AI Services (Google Gemini API)
To provide intelligent financial analysis and stock market insights, this App integrates the **Google Gemini API**. 

* **Data Transmitted:** Only text-based queries or financial data labels provided by the user are sent to the AI service. 
* **Provider:** Google LLC (Gemini API).
* **Purpose:** To generate automated summaries and insights.
* **User Consent:** The App requires explicit user permission via an in-app prompt **before** any data is transmitted to the Gemini API. 
* **Data Handling:** No personally identifiable information (PII) is shared with Google.

## 3. Financial Data Handling
All financial data (stock codes, prices, quantities) is stored locally on your device or within your private iCloud storage. We do not have access to this data.
